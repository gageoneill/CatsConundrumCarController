W - moves all 4 motors forwards
A - moves the right 2 motors forwards and the left 2 motors backwards to achieve an in-place skid steer left.
S - moves all  motors backwards
D - moves the left 2 motors backwards and the right 2 motors forwards to achieve an in-place skid steer right.
Q - stops all motor function

Additional Functionality:
1 - sets the motor throttle to 30% power
2 - sets the motor throttle to 60% power
3 - sets the motor throttle to 100% power (default)
I - achieves front wheel drive
O - achieves rear wheel drive
